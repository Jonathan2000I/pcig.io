History

The rich journey of PCIG as one of Nigeria's leading business group humbly began width the registration of Petterson Clor International Ltd as a single indigenious company.The company was incorprated on the 31st of July 1990 as a private limited liability company with CAC registration number RC 152661.Highly leveraging on the prestingious professional and enterpreneurial pedigree of its founding chairman and CEO Dr.Peter Dongo Isibor the company began business operation that same year.

Through the visionary and insightful leadership of the company's progentor, Dr Isbor the company grew in leaps and bounds in the company's initial core areas of engagement namely, Medical Procurement and Healthcare Turnkey projectdesign and management. Within 15 years of coming into existence the company had gained enough liquidity to futher diversify its operation into other sectors like energy and manpower development to explore and exploit emerging opportunites.

since its humble beginning 23 years ago the company has evolved into a leading proudly Nigerian business conglomerate with diverse intrest in energy,healthcare,agriculture,infrastructure,manpower and development initiatives(i.e Public Private partnership)PPP initiatives.
